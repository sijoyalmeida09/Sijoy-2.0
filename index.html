About JoSho IT
Named after the people who made it possible.

JoSho = John + Shobha. Named after my parents — the two people who gave me everything I needed to start building. I named my company after them, because everything I build is built on the foundation they gave me.

I started fixing laptops in Vasai, Maharashtra when I was 19. Moved to Boston for my MS at Northeastern. Saw a technician charge $250 for a 2-hour camera job. Started doing it better. Word of mouth spread. Now JoSho IT serves 50+ small businesses — convenience stores, restaurants, retail shops, PropTech startups — across Boston and Maine.

The dual capability is the thing nobody else can do: I can write a Python automation script and run Ethernet cable through a wall in the same afternoon.
